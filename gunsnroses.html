 A workshop by Kerala Police Department

A weapon, arm or armament is any device that can be used with intent to inflict damage or harm. Weapons are used to increase the efficacy and efficiency of activities such as hunting, crime, law enforcement, self-defence, and warfare. Head Constables who are promoted to ASI/SI ranks are also trained in Police Training college. In addition, in-service/reorientation/ capsule courses are conducted for officers of and above the rank of SI in Police Training College. The constable’s induction training is given in the Armed Police Battalions for 9 months.

This workshop focusses on awareness and safe use of weapons and arms. Eminent and well experienced police officers of Kerala Police Department is taking classes on the Guns, arms , body vests, etc which the police department generally use.

The class will be about :
· Working of guns.
· Assembly and disassembly of guns.
· Cleaning and maintenance of arms.
· Advanced technologies in weopons.
· Latest weapons used in practise.
· Safety features and protective use of armaments.
